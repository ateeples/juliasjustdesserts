Frequently Asked Questions


  • How far in advance should I contact you about an order?
  • I will usually respond to an order inquiry in 1-2 business days. Depending on how creative your order is, a cake would usually take 2-4 days, cupcakes 2-4 days, and cookies 1-2 days.
  • Do you use fondant in your cakes?
  • Nope! I shape the cake with frosting.
  • Are your cakes a custom design? Can I give input?
  • Absolutely! All my cakes are a custom design and input is very welcome. Depending on how difficult your idea is to achieve I would plan accordingly and place your order earlier rather than later.
  • What flavors can I choose?
  • For cake any regular flavor; vanilla, chocolate, confetti, red velvet, german chocolate, etc. For cookies I can do oatmeal, ginger bread, brown butter, sugar cookies, etc. I'm open to trying new things if you have a different idea!
  • How do you accept payment?
  • I accept payment through paypal, venmo, cashapp, cash or check.
